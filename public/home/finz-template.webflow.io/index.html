FAQs : Write in the customer's voice

Galactos provides online payment solutions, including credit card processing, digital wallets, and banking services.

How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started." Enter your details, verify your identity with a valid ID and phone number, and you're ready to start banking. Account creation takes less than 5 minutes.

Are my funds safe with Galactos?

Yes. We use 256-bit bank-level encryption and are fully regulated. Your deposits are insured up to $250,000, and we employ multi-factor authentication to protect your account.

How long do transfers take?

Instant transfers between Galactos users happen in seconds. External bank transfers typically take 1-3 business days depending on the receiving bank.

What are the fees for transactions?

Galactos-to-Galactos transfers are free. External transfers may have a small fee ($2-5) depending on your account tier. Check our pricing page for full details.

How do I apply for a loan?

Log in to your account, navigate to the "Loans" section, select your desired amount, and complete the application. Most loans are approved within 24 hours with funds disbursed immediately after approval.

Can I access my account on mobile?

Yes. Our website is fully responsive and works seamlessly on any devicedesktop, tablet, or smartphone. Simply log in through your mobile browser for full banking access on the go.

What if I forget my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen, verify your identity via email or SMS, and create a new password. For additional help, contact our 24/7 support team.

How do I contact customer support?

Reach us 24/7 via email at support@galactostrustbacorp.com, or call our hotline. Executive members have access to priority support and dedicated account managers.
